My contribution

A federated governance model that lets 195+ member states collaborate without data conflicts. Real time synchronization across distributed UN agencies. Performance tuning for a high traffic global policy platform. Security hardening for sensitive diplomatic data.

Serves as the technical backbone for global migration policy coordination, adopted by G7 delegations and UN decision makers as the primary reference tool for International Migration Review Forum deliberations.
